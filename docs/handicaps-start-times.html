Handicaps & Start Times

Handicaps and start times are calculated for 24 Sep 2022.

Handicaps are calculated for the full course using results from the current season and the previous 3 seasons.

Times from previous seasons are adjusted by adding 3 minutes. Start times are calculated by subtracting the adjusted best time from the target finish time of 7:30.
